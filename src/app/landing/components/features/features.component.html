Football League Simulation Features

The Football League Simulation offers a comprehensive set of features to create and manage your own football league. Here's a detailed overview of what you can do with this application.

  • Add up to 20 teams to your league
  • Remove teams (minimum 2 required)
  • Customize team names
  • Adjust team strengths (50-100) to affect match outcomes
  • Reset to default teams
  • Automatically generated fixtures for all teams
  • Play week-by-week or simulate the entire league at once
  • Match results based on team strengths with realistic randomization
  • Home advantage factor for more realistic outcomes
  • Reset the league at any time
  • Real-time updated league table
  • Complete team statistics (played, won, drawn, lost)
  • Goal tracking (goals for, against, difference)
  • Points calculation based on standard football rules
  • Tiebreakers for teams with equal points
  • Championship probability calculations (after week 4)
  • Visual indication of teams with no championship chance
  • Editable match results with automatic recalculation
  • Match history with complete results by week
  • Support for both even and odd numbers of teams
