Football League Simulation Tutorial

Welcome to the Football League Simulation tutorial! This guide will walk you through how to use the application to create and manage your own football league.

Getting Started

Setting Up Your League

When you first open the application, you'll see the team selection screen. Here you can:

  • Add teams (up to 20) using the "Add Team" button
  • Remove teams using the trash icon (minimum 2 teams required)
  • Customize team names by typing in the input fields
  • Adjust team strengths using the sliders (higher strength = better performance)

Pro Tip:

Team strength significantly affects match outcomes. A team with 100 strength will generally perform better than a team with 50 strength.

Once you're happy with your teams, click the "Start League" button to begin the simulation.

Playing the League

Navigating the League Interface

After starting the league, you'll see the main simulation interface with several sections:

League Controls

On the right side, you'll find the control panel with these options:

  • Next Week: Play the next round of matches
  • Play Entire League: Automatically play all remaining matches
  • Reset League: Start over with team selection

League Standings

The standings table shows:

  • Team positions based on points
  • Match statistics (played, won, drawn, lost)
  • Goal information (goals for, against, difference)
  • Total points

Teams with no chance of winning the championship will be highlighted in red.

Current Week Matches

Below the standings, you'll see the current week's matches with results after they're played.

Match History

As the league progresses, you'll see an accordion of past weeks and their results.

Note:

After the 4th week, championship probabilities will appear on the right side of the screen.

Editing Match Results

Customizing Match Outcomes

You can edit the results of matches that have already been played:

  1. Find the match you want to edit in the current week or match history
  2. Click the pencil icon next to the match
  3. In the dialog that appears, adjust the goals for each team
  4. Click "Save" to update the result

After editing a match result, the league standings will automatically recalculate to reflect the changes. This includes updating points, goal differences, and championship probabilities.

Pro Tip:

Use this feature to create "what if" scenarios or correct any results you feel were unfair!

League Rules

Understanding the League System

The league follows standard football rules:

  • Points System:
    • Win: 3 points
    • Draw: 1 point
    • Loss: 0 points
  • Standings Tiebreakers:
    1. Total points
    2. Goal difference (goals scored minus goals conceded)
    3. Goals scored
    4. Alphabetical order of team names
  • Fixture Generation: Each team plays against every other team twice (home and away)

Championship Determination

The team with the most points at the end of all matches is declared the champion. After the 4th week, the system will calculate and display championship probabilities for each team based on their current performance and remaining matches.

Note on Championship Probabilities:

These probabilities are calculated based on current standings, team strengths, and remaining matches. They're updated after each match and when results are edited.
